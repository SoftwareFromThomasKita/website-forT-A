About Us

T&A Remodeling and Renovations is Albany NY’s trusted home remodeling and renovation contractor. Built on a passion for transforming homes and businesses into beautiful, functional spaces, our experienced team delivers high-quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service on every project. With over 10 years of remodeling and renovation work right here in Albany, we let our history speak for itself.

Based in Albany NY, we proudly serve homeowners and businesses throughout the Capital Region — including Guilderland, Colonie, Troy, Schenectady, Latham, Clifton Park, and Cohoes. Whether you need a kitchen remodel, bathroom renovation, new flooring, or a fresh coat of paint, T&A is Albany’s go-to remodeling contractor.
