About Us

T&A Remodeling and Renovations started with a passion for transforming homes and businesses into beautiful, functional spaces. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing high-quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service. We've been renovating homes and remodeling rooms for over 10 years, so when it comes to professional opinions, we let our history speak for itself.

Based in Albany, NY, we proudly serve homeowners and businesses throughout the Capital Region — including Guilderland, Colonie, Troy, Schenectady, Latham, Clifton Park, and Cohoes.
